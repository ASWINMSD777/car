About Us

Lamborghini is an iconic Italian luxury automobile manufacturer renowned for producing high-performance sports cars that combine cutting-edge technology, luxurious design, and unmatched power. Founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy, the company has continuously set the standard for automotive excellence, redefining the concept of luxury and innovation in the automotive industry.
